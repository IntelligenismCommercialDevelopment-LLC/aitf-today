About
What is AITF.TODAY?

AITF.TODAY is a daily intelligence service covering the AI agent framework ecosystem. We track industry developments, research papers, architectural trends, and tooling advances across the agent technology landscape.

All published content uses the AITF v4.0 structured format — a protocol designed for both human readability and machine consumption. Every article explicitly marks its reasoning structure: conclusions, evidence, assumptions, risks, and knowledge gaps.

How It Works

Content is generated by an automated pipeline with human editorial oversight:

1. Signal collection from Hacker News, RSS feeds, and arXiv

2. Keyword filtering and LLM-based relevance assessment

3. Structured content generation in AITF v4 format

4. HTML rendering and static site publishing

The pipeline produces four content types: Briefs (short signal summaries), Analysis (deep-dive articles with full logic trees and agent commentary), Paper Candidates (curated daily research from arXiv), and Insights (human-authored editorial commentary).
